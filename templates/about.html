🔍 How DigiSign Works

🔑 Step 1: Generate Keys

Click "Generate Keys" to create a private and public key pair for signing and verifying documents.

✍️ Step 2: Sign a Document

Upload a document and click "Sign Document". The website will generate a digital signature file.

✅ Step 3: Verify Signature

Upload the original document and the signature file, then click "Verify Signature" to check if the document is authentic.
